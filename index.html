Cooling-off Period for Insurance Policies: Your Right to Change Your Mind

Many consumers purchase insurance policies only to later realize they want to cancel. Fortunately, Australian law provides a **cooling-off period**, allowing policyholders to **get a full refund within a certain timeframe**. At SD Insurance, we help you understand your rights.

How Long is the Cooling-off Period for Insurance?

The standard cooling-off period for most insurance policies is **14 to 30 days**, depending on the insurer.
